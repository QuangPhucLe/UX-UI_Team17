At Nike Shoes, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal data is protected. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and manage your information while you interact with our website.

Who We Are and What This Policy Covers

Nike Shoes is dedicated to providing an exceptional shopping experience and protecting your personal information. This policy covers data collected through your interactions on our website, including when you browse products, make purchases, and subscribe to our communications. By using our site, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information to enhance your shopping experience, provide personalized service, and communicate with you effectively. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, mailing address, and phone number.
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment details, which are handled securely by our payment processor.
  • Browsing Data: Information about how you use our site, including the pages you visit, products you view, and your IP address. This data helps us improve site functionality and product offerings.

We may also collect information through cookies, which help personalize your shopping experience and remember your preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders efficiently.
  • Provide customer support and communicate updates on your order status.
  • Customize your experience by recommending products that may interest you.
  • Improve our website and product offerings based on customer interactions.
  • Send promotional offers, newsletters, or updates (if you have opted in).

We are committed to protecting your information and employ secure methods to store your data. Our website follows industry standards to prevent unauthorized access and ensure your data's safety.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us through our support page.

For additional details or questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us.
